Built for regulated environments

NAIC · HIPAA · GLBA · Human-in-the-Loop by design

NAIC AI Model Bulletin

ECO-AI never makes unilateral coverage denials. Every output carries citations to source documents and requires licensed adjuster authorization for binding decisions.

HIPAA / GLBA Data Privacy

PII and PHI are not logged or repeated unnecessarily. Claims are referenced by ID. Enterprise deployments should ring-fence data from foundational model training.

Prompt Injection Protection

Structured MCP tool schemas and explicit output formats reduce hallucination risk and limit the attack surface for adversarial inputs in claim narratives.

Four-stage claims workflow

ECO-AI follows the same lifecycle structure as a human adjuster — but at machine speed.

STAGE 01

FNOL & Triage

Policy verification, fraud scoring, document gap analysis, and routing to FAST_TRACK or COMPLEX_ADJUDICATION.

STAGE 02

Investigation

Multimodal document parsing, chronological timeline construction, and cross-referencing for narrative inconsistencies.

STAGE 03

Policy Analysis

Evidence / Policy Clause / Analysis / Conclusion structured output for every coverage question an adjuster needs to answer.

STAGE 04

Settlement

Damage aggregation, reserve calculation, compliant communication drafts, and adjuster-gated status updates.
