# ClaimGPT Demo Script

Use this for the OpenAI Platform "Demo Recording URL" requirement.

## Video metadata

- Title: `ClaimGPT demo for OpenAI app review`
- Description: `Developer Mode demo of ClaimGPT OCCIE casualty analysis and workspace rendering.`

## Recording target

Keep the demo short: 60 to 90 seconds.

## Prompt to paste

```text
Analyze this bodily injury claim: A claimant reports a rear-end collision on 2026-06-14 with ambulance transport, neck and lumbar pain, missed work, and an urgent payout request. The police report suggests shared fault, an ER note and PT intake are attached, and the story changed after family review. Please evaluate severity, TIP, litigation risk, fraud risk, and show the ClaimGPT OCCIE workspace.
```

## Recording flow

1. Show ClaimGPT connected in ChatGPT Developer Mode.
2. Paste the sample prompt.
3. Show that `analyze_claim` runs and returns executive status plus structured casualty analysis.
4. Show that `render_claim_workspace` loads the ClaimGPT OCCIE workspace.
5. Briefly show the production privacy page at `https://claim-gpt.com/privacy`.
6. Briefly show the production support page at `https://claim-gpt.com/support`.

## Suggested narration

`This is ClaimGPT running in ChatGPT Developer Mode. It performs a read-only OCCIE-style casualty review, estimating severity, total incurred prediction, litigation pressure, fraud risk, medical chronology, and next-best actions for the adjuster. Here the app reviews a bodily injury collision claim, returns the executive status and red flags, and then displays the full workspace. The production privacy and support pages are live on claim-gpt.com.`
